Project Overview

MedConnect required a secure cross-platform mobile app to link patients with healthcare providers through video consultations, appointment scheduling, medical records, and prescription tracking. We developed a HIPAA-compliant solution with React Native that empowers patients to manage their care while equipping providers with efficient, mobile-first tools.

Key Features

  • Video/audio telemedicine consultations
  • Appointment booking & management
  • Electronic health records (EHR)
  • Prescription management & reminders
  • Secure messaging with providers
  • HIPAA-compliant data security
