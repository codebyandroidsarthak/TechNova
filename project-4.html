Project Overview

EduVerse needed a scalable learning management system to support online and hybrid education for thousands of students with course management, live classes, and performance tracking. We delivered a cloud-based platform featuring live video sessions, assignment submissions, automated grading, and advanced analytics for educators and administrators.

Key Features

  • Course creation and management
  • Live video classes (WebRTC)
  • Assignment submission and grading
  • Student performance analytics
  • Discussion forums and messaging
  • Certificate generation
